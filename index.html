ABOUT AZAD-1

Student Nanosatellite Project

Azad-1 is an ambitious project to develop and launch a Nano-satellite developed by Undergraduate students of MANIT, Bhopal, India. The project was started in April 2012 with a team of 40 Undergraduate Engineering students from different streams. This project envisions excelling our institute MANIT Bhopal into realms of Space Science and Technology Research by motivating students to work on development of student satellite.
